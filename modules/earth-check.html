NOAA GOES-16 East

Stationed at 75.2°W above the equator, GOES-16 monitors the Americas and Atlantic Ocean. Its Advanced Baseline Imager captures stunning GeoColor imagery every 10 minutes at 1808x1808 resolution, providing critical data for weather forecasting and hurricane tracking.

🌊

NOAA GOES-17 West

Positioned at 137.2°W, GOES-17 watches over the Pacific Ocean and Western Americas. Working in tandem with GOES-16, it provides continuous full-disc imagery of weather systems, wildfires, volcanic eruptions, and atmospheric conditions across the Pacific basin.
